Movement limited by movement speed
Movement is spread across one Combat Turn. All Initiative Passes within that turn share the one movement speed value.
Complex Action max. 1/INI Pass
You can take either a Complex Action OR two Simple Actions, not both. You may also take a Free Action in the same Action Phase as your Complex Action.
Simple Action max. 2/INI Pass
A character may take two Simple Actions, though only one can be an attack action.
Free Action 1/INI Pass
A character may take one Free Action during their Action Phase or at some later point in the Initiative Pass.
Interrupt Action limited by INI Score
Take a specific action out of turn, at the cost of Initiative score points.
Condition
Conditions alter your capabilities in a variety of ways, and can arise as a result of a spell, an attack, a toxin, or other effect.
Dice Pool Modifiers
Environmental modifiers reflect the conditions surrounding the action.
Situational modifiers cover things the character may be doing while attacking and also effects the character might be under when they attack.
